WILD ANIMALS AND CITIES OF AFRICA

Lion: A lion is one of the most powerful wild animals found in Africa.

Primarily eat large herbivores such as zebras, antelopes, and buffaloes.

Crocodile: A crocodile is a large aquatic reptile known for its powerful jaws.

Crocodiles live in rivers, lakes, and wetlands throughout Africa.
